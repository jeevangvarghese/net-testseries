Sample Question

Teaching Aptitude — Sectional Test Preview
Which of the following best describes the concept of "Flipped Classroom" as a teaching approach?
A
Students attend class in the morning and complete assignments in the evening under teacher supervision.
B
The teacher delivers lectures while students take notes and ask questions at the end of the session.
C
Students review instructional content at home and use classroom time for problem-solving and discussions.
D
Students evaluate teacher performance and the teacher evaluates student performance simultaneously.
Answer: C — Correct. The Flipped Classroom model inverts the traditional learning structure. Students first encounter new material outside the classroom (typically via video lectures or reading) and then use in-class time for deeper learning activities, discussions, and problem-solving with teacher guidance. This allows for more active and personalised learning during class time.
